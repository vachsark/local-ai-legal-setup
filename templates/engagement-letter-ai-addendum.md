## 3. What AI Will NOT Do in Your Matter

Regardless of the tools used, [FIRM NAME] does not permit AI to:

- **Provide final legal conclusions or recommendations** — all legal judgments are made by a licensed attorney
- **File documents with any court or agency** — all filings are independently reviewed and verified by an attorney before submission
- **Communicate directly with you** — AI-generated text is never sent to you without attorney review and approval
- **Generate or verify legal citations for use in court submissions** — all citations are independently verified in authoritative legal databases (Westlaw, Lexis, or official government sources)
- **Process your data through unapproved third-party services** — only tools listed in Section 1 above are used

## 4. Data Handling and Confidentiality

### 4.1 Local AI Tools (No Cloud Transmission)

[Select this section if using local-only tools and delete Section 4.2, or retain both]

[FIRM NAME]'s AI tools run entirely on our own hardware. This means:

- **Your data never leaves our machines.** Documents, facts, and communications processed by AI tools are not transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or AI provider.
- **No internet connection is used during AI processing.** All inference runs locally after the one-time model download during setup.
- **No training on your data.** Local models are static — your matter information is never used to update or retrain any AI model.
- **All services are restricted to firm devices only** (bound to localhost), inaccessible from outside the firm's own hardware even on a local network.

### 4.2 Cloud AI Tools (Data Leaves the Firm)

[Select this section if using any cloud AI tools and delete Section 4.1, or retain both]

- A signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is in place between [FIRM NAME] and [PROVIDER NAME]
- Transmission is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+

**By signing this addendum, you consent** to the transmission of your matter information to [PROVIDER NAME] for the tasks listed in Section 2 above.

## 5. Attorney Supervision of AI Outputs

Every AI-generated work product used in your matter is reviewed by a licensed attorney before use. [FIRM NAME]'s supervision process:

1. **Review** — The attorney reads the AI output for accuracy, legal soundness, and completeness
2. **Log** — The outcome (approved, modified, or rejected) is recorded in [FIRM NAME]'s compliance system, along with any attorney notes on changes made
3. **Finalize** — Only reviewed output is incorporated into work product or communications

This process is designed to comply with ABA Formal Opinion 512 (2024), which requires attorneys to supervise and take responsibility for AI outputs before they are used. Monthly compliance reports are generated and retained.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding AI use in your matter:

**Right to Opt Out.** You may decline AI assistance in your matter at any time by written notice to [ATTORNEY NAME]. [FIRM NAME] will accommodate this request. Opting out may affect turnaround times and fees for affected tasks.

**Right to Ask.** You may ask at any time which specific deliverables in your matter involved AI assistance and what verification steps were taken.

**Right to Information.** You may request a summary of how AI tools were used in your matter at the conclusion of representation.
